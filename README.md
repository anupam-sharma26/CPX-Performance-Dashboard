# CPX Affiliate Performance Dashboard

## Overview

This dashboard analyzes affiliate marketing performance across clients, affiliates, coupons, and campaigns. It provides insights into revenue, commissions, payouts, and profitability to help marketing and business teams understand affiliate effectiveness and campaign results.

## Business Objective

The goal of this dashboard is to help teams:

* Monitor affiliate marketing performance across clients
* Track revenue, commission, payout, and profit metrics
* Identify top-performing affiliates and clients
* Analyze coupon performance and campaign effectiveness
* Understand order trends and customer behavior

## Tools Used

Power BI
Power Query
DAX
SQL / Data Transformation

## Key Metrics

Net Orders – Total completed orders generated through affiliate campaigns
Net Revenue – Total revenue generated through affiliate channels
Net Commission – Commission earned from campaigns
Net Payout – Amount paid to affiliates
Net Profit – Profit after commissions and payouts
Active Affiliates – Total affiliates participating in campaigns

## Dashboard Insights

* Ounass generates the highest net revenue among clients.
* Sephora contributes the highest net profit among top clients.
* Returning customers generate the majority of orders.
* A small number of coupons drive a large share of campaign revenue.
* Top affiliates contribute significantly to total revenue and commission.

## Business Value

This dashboard helps teams:

* Monitor affiliate profitability
* Identify high-performing affiliates
* Optimize coupon and campaign strategies
* Track revenue and commission trends
* Support data-driven marketing decisions
